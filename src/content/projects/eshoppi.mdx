eShoopi is a data-driven platform designed to help B2B businesses in Bangladesh understand their customers better using their existing sales data.

Most businesses already have valuable data — invoices, POS records, order histories — but it remains unstructured and underutilized. eShoopi transforms that raw data into a structured, actionable customer intelligence layer.

---

## Problem

B2B companies often face challenges such as:

- No clear understanding of their most valuable customers
- Random or inefficient sales outreach
- Lack of visibility into customer behavior and purchase patterns
- Missed opportunities due to unstructured or messy data

---

## Solution

eShoopi processes and structures raw business data to generate meaningful insights:

- Clean and standardized customer datasets
- Unified customer profiles from fragmented sources
- Segmentation based on behavior and purchasing patterns
- Actionable insights for sales and marketing teams

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Customer Profiling**
  Build detailed customer profiles based on transaction history and behavior

- **Segmentation**
  Identify high-value, inactive, and growth-potential customers

- **Purchase Pattern Analysis**
  Understand what customers buy, how often, and when

- **Data Cleaning Pipeline**
  Automatically clean, normalize, and structure messy datasets

---

## Business Impact

- More effective and targeted sales outreach
- Reduced wasted effort on low-value leads
- Better customer retention strategies
- Improved decision-making using real data
